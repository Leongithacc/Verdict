Verdict — Quick start (portable)
=============================

1. Unzip this folder anywhere (Desktop is fine). No installer.
2. Run WPEP.exe (the Verdict app)
   - Windows SmartScreen will warn about an unknown publisher: this is
     expected for an unsigned open-source tool. Click "More info" →
     "Run anyway". Verify the zip's SHA256 against the published hash
     if you want certainty.
3. First launch shows a welcome screen. Click "Scan my system" — the scan
   is read-only and needs no administrator.
4. To MEASURE (frametime benchmarks) or run DIAGNOSTICS (DPC/ISR):
   - click "Relaunch as administrator" when prompted (ETW is admin-only
     by Windows design)
   - Measure will offer to download Intel PresentMon (~10 MB, pinned
     version, SHA256-verified). Nothing is downloaded without your click.
   - On a laptop: plug in the charger. Benchmarks on battery are blocked
     (power throttling makes them meaningless).
5. Everything Verdict saves (runs, reports, settings) stays in this folder.
   Uninstall = delete the folder.

Verdict never modifies your system. It measures, diagnoses and recommends —
and tells you honestly when a tweak does nothing.

CLI for advanced users: wpep-cli.exe (run from a terminal).
